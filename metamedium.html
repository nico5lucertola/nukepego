The Videogame Metamedium

Virtual worlds within videogames contain books, paintings, newspapers, radios and other media artifacts that take part in the gaming experience in different ways. These objects can be employed as just ornamental elements in the background but, in some cases, they function as fundamental pieces for the video game’s narrative and interactivity. In my master thesis, I tried to dissect this phenomenon both from a semiotics and game design point of view.

Written in 2019-2020.
